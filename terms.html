What the app does

Spirit Box is a paranormal investigation utility. It uses your device microphone to run audio sessions, detect EVP-style sound activity, and capture session evidence (voice count, peak readings, activity timeline). Sessions can be replayed and shared from your device. The app is for entertainment and exploration of paranormal-themed audio play.
