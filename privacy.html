Privacy Policy

Cloud Motion Lab · Last updated May 4, 2026

Spirit Box is built to run paranormal-themed audio sessions on your device and keep your sessions private. This page explains exactly what the app does and does not collect.

What we collect

Nothing personal. The app does not require an account. It does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising purposes.

Microphone audio

Spirit Box requires microphone access to run a session. Audio is captured and processed on your device for activity detection and optional session recording. Recordings, session cards, voice counts, peak readings, and activity timelines are stored locally on your device. They are not transmitted to a Cloud Motion Lab server, are not stored remotely, and are not shared with any third party. If you delete the app, your saved sessions are deleted with it. The only time session content leaves your device is when you explicitly tap the share button to send a session card or recording through the iOS share sheet, and in that case the destination is whichever app you choose.

Anonymous analytics

The app uses TelemetryDeck for anonymous, privacy-preserving usage analytics. TelemetryDeck collects coarse, non-identifying signals (such as which screen was viewed, whether a session was started, app version, device type, country) hashed with a daily rotating salt. No persistent device identifier is stored. You can read TelemetryDeck's privacy approach at telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

In-app purchases

Spirit Box offers an optional Pro upgrade as either a weekly auto-renewing subscription with a free introductory trial or a one-time lifetime purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple's App Store. Spirit Box receives only an Apple-issued receipt confirming entitlement. We do not see your Apple ID, your payment details, or your billing address.

Rewarded ads

Spirit Box may show optional rewarded video ads when you choose to continue a session without paying. These ads are served by Unity Ads. Ad-network SDKs may collect coarse device signals (device model, OS version, IP address truncated to country level, anonymized advertising identifier where permitted) to deliver relevant ads and prevent fraud. Spirit Box does not pass any personal information to ad networks. You can opt out by skipping the ad-gated feature and either declining the ad or paying for Pro. We do not request App Tracking Transparency permission and do not track you across other apps or websites.

What we do not do

  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of any personal data
  • No remote storage of audio recordings or session evidence
  • No background location, no contacts, no camera

Children

Spirit Box is themed around paranormal entertainment. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from any user, including children, and complies with COPPA by collecting no personal information.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, the "Last updated" date above will move and we will note the change in the app's What's New on its next release.
